Buffalo Chicken Dip

Have you been craving some buffalo goodness? This is the perfect recipe to fulfil your tastebuds and share with others. Follow the steps below to endulge in spicey cheese heaven.

Prep Time: 20 minutes, Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

    Meat
  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of Frank's hot buffalo sauce
    • Produce
  • ½ tablespoon garlic powder
  • ¼ tablespoon onion powder
    • Condiments
  • ½ cup of bleu cheese dressing
  • ⅓ cup of mayonnaise
  • ½ cup ranch dressing
    • Baking & Spices
  • ½ tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 pinch of salt & pepper
    • Dairy
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • ¾ cup of monterey jack cheese
  • ¾ cup of mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup of sour cream

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 365℉
  2. Cook chicken and shred into small pieces
  3. Combine all ingredients and spoon into baking dish
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until mixture is heated through
  5. Serve with chips, crackers, pretzels, veggies, and anything else you can think of.

For larger parties double the amount of each ingredient. For hot and spicey lovers add as much buffalo sauce as you'd like!

Enjoy!
